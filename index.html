      • Navigate to the printer’s control panel using the touchscreen or buttons available.
    2. Go to Network Settings:
      • Find the network settings or connectivity options in the menu.
    3. View Connected Devices:
      • Look for an option that shows connected devices or a list of clients. This might be under a section labeled "Network," "Wi-Fi," or "Bluetooth," depending on how the phones are connected.
    4. Disconnect Devices:
      • Select the device (phone) you wish to disconnect and look for an option to disconnect or remove the device from the printer.
    5. Confirm Changes:
      • Make sure to confirm any prompts to finalize the disconnection.
